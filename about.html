What is KENTECH Study Hub?

KENTECH Study Hub is a web-based study management platform designed for students at Korea Institute of Energy Technology. It lets you track tasks, monitor your academic progress, and stay motivated throughout the semester — all without needing an account or a backend server.

Everything is saved locally in your browser, so your data stays private and the site loads instantly.

Core Features

  • ✅ Add, edit, and delete study tasks
  • 📊 Real-time progress bar per subject and overall
  • 🗂️ Subject categories (Math, Physics, CS, …)
  • 📅 Due dates with overdue detection
  • 🔴 Priority levels (High / Medium / Low)
  • 🌙 Dark mode toggle
  • 💬 Daily motivational quotes

Tech Stack

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JavaScript localStorage API Google Fonts Vercel
